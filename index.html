Frequently Asked Questions

What are your prices? ‚ñº
Our dog sitting service is $45 per night, which includes comprehensive care: walking, feeding, playtime, daily photo and video updates, cuddles, and full access to our spacious backyard where your dog can run, play, and relax throughout their stay.
Do you do short-term and long-term stays? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We accommodate both short-term stays (a night or a weekend) and long-term stays (weeks or longer). Whether you're going away for a quick trip or an extended vacation, your furry friend is welcome to stay with us for as long as needed.
What should I bring for my dog? ‚ñº
Please bring the following items to ensure your dog feels comfortable and at home:
  • Required: Food, food and water bowls, leash, poop bags, and any medication (if applicable)
  • Important: Veterinarian contact information and pet insurance details
  • Optional: Favorite toys, bed or blanket, and wipes for easy cleanup
Having familiar items helps your dog feel more comfortable during their stay!
Do you offer shower or grooming services? ‚ñº
No, we do not currently offer bathing or grooming services. We focus on providing excellent care, playtime, exercise, and lots of love for your dog. We recommend scheduling grooming appointments with a professional groomer before or after your dog's stay with us.
Can you pet sit at our house? ‚ñº
Unfortunately, no. At this time, we only offer dog sitting services at our home. This allows us to provide the best possible care in a safe, controlled environment where your dog can enjoy our big backyard and receive constant attention and supervision.
Why did you start this business? ‚ñº
I started MenloBark because I genuinely love dogs and find so much joy in caring for them while their owners are away. Knowing that families can relax on their trips while their furry friends are happy and well-cared-for brings me great satisfaction. I'm also an entrepreneur at heart, so I combined my passion for dogs with my love of business to create something meaningful. Every dog that stays with me becomes part of my extended family!
